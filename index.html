About the Conference

The ICEECT-2024 organized by Department of Electrical Electronics and Communication Engineering, Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology, India. The conference is technically sponsored by IEEE Uttar Pradesh Section, India. Uttar Pradesh Section is located in Region 10 and is represented at the India Council. IEEE UP Section interfaces with industries and academia through various technical and humanitarian activities. Conference Proceedings will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore.

  • To provide an opportunity to the practicing engineers, academicians, and researchers to meet in a forum to discuss various issues and its future direction in the field of Electrical, Electronics and Computing Technologies with the following objectives:
  • To foster the exchange of research advances and emerging technologies.
  • To organize expert's talk by domain experts of global eminence.
  • To recognize the eminent global achievers for their remarkable achievement and contributions in their respective domains by Galgotias awards of excellence.

About Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology

Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology is a leading educational institution based at Greater Noida, Delhi NCR, India. The College has established itself as a high-quality education provider with prime focus on holistic learning and imbibing competitive abilities in students.

The College is approved by AICTE and prides itself in being a multi-discipline campus in the NCR, spread over a large area and equipped with world class facilities.

Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology promises to become one of India's leading colleges with an acknowledged reputation for excellence in research and teaching. With its outstanding faculty, world class teaching standards, and innovative academic programs, Galgotias intends to set a new benchmark in the Indian education system.

About the Department of Electrical, Electronics & Communication Engineering

Department of Electrical Electronics and Communication Engineering (EECE) shares the vision and mission of Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology in imparting quality education to students at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels to meet the national and global requirements by providing a stimulating and flexible learning environment. The Department of EECE is one of the premier departments of the School of Engineering and Technology, Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology. Both the undergraduate programs (Electrical & Electronics Engineering and Electronics & Communication Engineering) are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation(NBA).
